I also love sports, basketball in particular. I love how abstract the game is in terms of offense and defense. In basketball obstacles can be overcome in many ways no matter which side you’re playing on. I view building websites the same way. I can understand that there’s not just one solution to a problem. There are many ways to solve a problem but I like solving it in the most efficient way possible to remove clutter. I have worked in retail and did online shopping of my own so I can help tackle the consumers interests even from the developers side. I understand what would attract a typical consumer to come to a website and what features would make it a flawless experience. My experience in retail has helped me build my skills in communicating with a large team as well as customer service. Im able to ask for help when needed and even lead when the opportunity arises. With these soft skills and my newfound technical skills ,I’m confident I can integrate my self and skills seamlessly in any workspace.
